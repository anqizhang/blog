Softly 隐私政策

生效日期：2026 年 2 月 24 日

Softly（以下简称"我们"）非常重视您的隐私。本隐私政策说明我们如何收集、使用和保护您在使用 Softly 应用时的信息。

1. 我们收集的信息

账号信息：Softly 支持通过 Google 账号或 Apple ID 登录。登录时，我们通过 Firebase Authentication 服务接收以下信息：

  • 用户唯一标识符（UID）
  • 您的显示名称（如 Google/Apple 账号提供）
  • 邮箱地址（如 Google/Apple 账号提供）

这些信息仅用于身份验证，不会用于营销、广告或与第三方共享。

快乐记录：您在 Softly 中创建的所有快乐记录（包括文字内容、心情等级、对话历史、AI 生成的插画）均存储在您的设备本地，使用 Apple SwiftData 框架保存。我们无法访问这些数据。

2. 第三方服务

Firebase Authentication（Google）：用于账号登录与身份验证。Firebase 可能收集设备信息用于安全防护（如防止滥用）。详见 Firebase 隐私政策。

Google Gemini AI：Softly 使用 Gemini AI 提供以下功能：

  • AI 对话：您的对话内容会发送至 Google Gemini API 以生成回复
  • 内容分类与描述：快速记录模式下，文字内容发送至 Gemini API 生成角色描述
  • 角色插画生成：基于记录内容生成卡通角色插画

重要说明：

  • 数据仅在使用上述功能时传输，且仅用于生成即时响应
  • 我们不会将您的数据用于训练 AI 模型
  • Google Gemini API 的数据处理受 Google AI 服务条款约束
  • 本地快速分类（关键词匹配）完全在设备上完成，不涉及网络传输

3. 相册访问

Softly 会在您选择分享或保存快乐卡片时，请求仅写入相册的权限（Photo Library Add Only）。我们不会读取您的相册内容。

4. 数据追踪与广告

Softly 不包含任何追踪器或广告。

  • 不使用 App Tracking Transparency (ATT)
  • 不收集设备标识符（IDFA/IDFV）
  • 不使用第三方分析工具（如 Firebase Analytics、Amplitude 等）
  • 不展示任何广告

5. 数据存储与安全

  • 所有快乐记录存储在您的 iPhone 本地，不会同步到云端
  • 账号认证信息由 Firebase Authentication 安全管理
  • 退出登录时，设备上的所有本地数据将被清除
  • 删除 App 将永久删除所有本地数据
  • 所有网络通信使用 HTTPS 加密传输

6. 账号删除

您可以随时在 App 内的「账号」页面删除您的账号。删除账号将：

  • 永久删除设备上的所有快乐记录
  • 删除 Firebase 中的账号认证信息
  • 此操作不可撤销

7. 儿童隐私

Softly 不针对 13 岁以下的儿童设计。我们不会有意收集儿童的个人信息。

8. 隐私政策变更

如果我们对本隐私政策进行重大修改，会在 App 内通知您。建议您定期查看本页面以了解最新信息。

Softly Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 24, 2026

Softly ("we") takes your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Softly app.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: Softly supports signing in with Google or Apple ID. During sign-in, we receive the following information through Firebase Authentication:

  • Unique user identifier (UID)
  • Display name (as provided by your Google/Apple account)
  • Email address (as provided by your Google/Apple account)

This information is used solely for authentication and is not used for marketing, advertising, or shared with third parties.

Joy Records: All joy records you create in Softly (including text content, mood levels, conversation history, and AI-generated illustrations) are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We cannot access this data.

2. Third-Party Services

Firebase Authentication (Google): Used for account sign-in and identity verification. Firebase may collect device information for security purposes (e.g., abuse prevention). See Firebase Privacy Policy.

Google Gemini AI: Softly uses Gemini AI for the following features:

  • AI Conversations: Your conversation content is sent to Google Gemini API to generate responses
  • Content Classification: In quick record mode, your text is sent to Gemini API to generate character descriptions
  • Character Illustrations: Cartoon character illustrations are generated based on your content

Important:

  • Data is only transmitted when using these features and solely for generating immediate responses
  • We do not use your data to train AI models
  • Google Gemini API data processing is governed by Google AI Terms of Service
  • Local classification (keyword matching) is performed entirely on-device with no network transmission

3. Photo Library Access

Softly requests add-only access to your photo library when you choose to share or save joy cards. We do not read your photo library contents.

4. Tracking and Advertising

Softly contains no trackers or advertisements.

  • No App Tracking Transparency (ATT) usage
  • No device identifier collection (IDFA/IDFV)
  • No third-party analytics (Firebase Analytics, Amplitude, etc.)
  • No advertisements

5. Data Storage and Security

  • All joy records are stored locally on your iPhone and are not synced to the cloud
  • Account authentication is securely managed by Firebase Authentication
  • Signing out clears all local data from the device
  • Deleting the app permanently deletes all local data
  • All network communication uses HTTPS encryption

6. Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from the Account page within the app. Deleting your account will:

  • Permanently delete all joy records on your device
  • Delete your authentication information from Firebase
  • This action is irreversible

7. Children's Privacy

Softly is not designed for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we make significant changes to this Privacy Policy, we will notify you within the app. We recommend checking this page periodically for updates.
